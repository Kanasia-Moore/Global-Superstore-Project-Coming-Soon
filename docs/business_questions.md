# Business Questions

This analysis aims to evaluate sales performance, profitability, and return behavior across the Global Superstore dataset.

---

## 1. Overall Business Performance

- How much revenue and profit is the business generating?
- What is the overall profit margin?
- Are we operating profitably at scale?

---

## 2. Returns Analysis

- What percentage of orders are being returned?
- How do returns impact overall profitability?
- Are certain segments, regions, or categories driving higher return rates?

---

## 3. Discount Strategy

- How are discounts distributed across orders?
- Do higher discounts lead to lower profit margins?
- Are there discount ranges that negatively impact profitability?

---

## 4. Profitability Drivers

- Which orders are most profitable?
- How does profit margin vary across different orders?
- Are there patterns where high sales do not translate to high profit?

---

## 5. Operational & Regional Insights

- How does performance vary by region?
- Are certain regions associated with higher returns or lower profitability?
- Can regional ownership (people table) provide additional context?

---

## Goal of Analysis

The goal of this project is to identify key drivers of profitability and returns, evaluate the effectiveness of discount strategies, and provide insights that could improve operational and financial performance.
